About Me

As a passionate fourth-year IT student, I am actively seeking internship opportunities to apply my skills and contribute to meaningful projects. Throughout my academic journey, I have cultivated strong cooperative skills, including a love for teamwork and collaboration. My ability to multitask effectively allows me to thrive in dynamic environments.
During my previous internship at the Madhya Pradesh State Electronics and Development Corporation, I had the privilege of being recognized as the “Best Intern of the Month.” In this role, I prepared and presented detailed reports for senior management, highlighting key findings and recommendations. Collaborating with cross-functional teams, I analyzed data to identify trends and insights. Additionally, I spearheaded the development of wireframes using industry-standard tools like Axure, translating business requirements into intuitive user interfaces. This experience not only honed my technical skills but also emphasized the importance of effective communication and stakeholder engagement.
Apart from my internship, I have also delved into Kajabi development, designing custom themes and templates to create visually appealing and user-friendly websites. Integrating third-party plugins further enhanced the functionality of Kajabi platforms. Collaborating closely with clients, I ensured personalized solutions aligned with their specific requirements.
Currently pursuing an Integrated Master’s in Computer Application from Devi Ahilya Vishwavidhyalaya, Indore, I have a solid foundation in programming languages such as Java, C++, and Python. My coursework also covers database management, software engineering, and web development.
Outside of the tech world, I am passionate about learning Spanish and exploring new horizons. Whether it’s solving complex problems or creating elegant web designs, I approach every challenge with enthusiasm and a growth mindset.
Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn or explore my portfolio here. Let’s collaborate and make a positive impact together.
